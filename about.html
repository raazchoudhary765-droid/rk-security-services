Built on verification, discipline and accountability

RK Security Services was founded to give Rajasthan's businesses and institutions a security partner they could hold to enterprise standards — not just a manpower vendor.

Our Story

A regional company, run like a national one

RK Security Services was established in Jaipur with a simple premise: security manpower should be as accountable as any other professional service. Under the leadership of Managing Director Raj Kumar, the company built its entire operating model around three pillars — verification, training and supervision — before taking on its first large contract.

Today, we deploy trained personnel across industrial plants, hospitals, corporate campuses, banks, malls, educational institutions and residential communities throughout Rajasthan, backed by a recruitment pipeline that treats police verification and structured training as non-negotiable, not optional extras.

Our Mission

To provide verified, disciplined and well-trained security personnel that clients can deploy with complete confidence, backed by consistent daily reporting and rapid support.

Our Vision

To be Rajasthan's most trusted name in security manpower — recognised for the same discipline and accountability found in the region's leading enterprises.
