Home Recruitment Process

Eight checkpoints before anyone reaches your gate

Every guard we deploy — regardless of role or urgency — passes through the same eight-step pipeline. No exceptions.

1. Application

Candidates apply and complete an initial screening interview covering background and physical fitness.

2. Document Verification

Identity, address and educational documents are collected and independently verified.

3. Police Verification

Formal police verification is filed and cleared before any candidate is eligible for deployment.

4. Training

Structured induction on conduct, patrolling protocol, reporting formats and emergency response.

5. Uniform

Candidates are issued standardised uniforms and undergo grooming and presentation checks.

6. Deployment

Cleared personnel are matched to a site based on role, risk profile and client requirement.

7. Supervisor Monitoring

Field supervisors conduct scheduled and surprise visits throughout the deployment.

8. Client Feedback

Regular feedback cycles with the client keep every deployment accountable and improving.
