How RK Security Services collects, uses and protects the information you share with us.

1. Information We Collect

When you request a quote, apply for a position, or contact us through this website, we may collect your name, phone number, email address, company or site name, and any details you choose to share about your security requirement or job application.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to quote requests and general enquiries
  • To process job applications and contact candidates
  • To coordinate deployment logistics with existing clients
  • To improve our services and website experience

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. Information is shared only with our internal operations and recruitment teams as necessary to fulfil your request.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect the information you provide. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

5. Your Choices

You may contact us at any time at rkservices966@gmail.com to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information held by us.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
