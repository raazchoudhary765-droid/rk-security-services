01 · Uniformed Deployment

Security Guard

Uniformed, trained guards providing access control, patrolling and asset protection for facilities of any size, deployed on fixed, rotating or night-shift schedules.

Responsibilities
  • Access control and visitor screening
  • Perimeter and interior patrolling
  • Incident logging and daily reporting
  • Basic fire and emergency response
Typical Industries
  • Industrial plants & factories
  • Corporate offices
  • Residential societies
  • Warehouses & malls
Benefits
  • Police-verified, uniformed personnel
  • Flexible shift and headcount scaling
  • Daily attendance and incident reports
  • Rapid replacement on request
Deployment Process
  • Requirement Assessment: We review your site, risk profile and shift needs.
  • Personnel Matching: Verified staff matching the role are shortlisted for your site.
  • Briefing & Deployment: Selected staff are briefed on site protocol before deployment.
  • Ongoing Supervision: Field supervisors monitor performance from day one.
Frequently Asked Questions
Is the Security Guard role covered by police verification?

Yes. Every candidate for this role undergoes the same police and background verification as all our other personnel before deployment.

How quickly can Security Guard staff be deployed?

Standard deployment is scheduled within 24–48 hours, with urgent requests accommodated wherever possible.
