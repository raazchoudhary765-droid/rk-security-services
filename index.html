FAQ

Common questions before you deploy

Are your guards police verified?

Yes. Every guard undergoes formal police verification and background checks before being cleared for deployment on any client site.

How quickly can you deploy staff?

Standard deployment is scheduled within 24–48 hours. Urgent replacements are typically dispatched within a few hours of a request.

Which areas do you currently serve?

We are headquartered in Jagatpura, Jaipur, and serve clients across Rajasthan, including industrial, commercial and residential sites.

Do you provide armed personnel?

Yes, licensed gunmen and civil gunmen are available for high-risk sites such as banks, cash-in-transit and industrial facilities.

How is site performance monitored?

Dedicated field supervisors conduct scheduled and surprise site visits, with daily attendance and incident reporting shared with clients.
